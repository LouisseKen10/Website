KNOW MORE ABOUT US

Get to know how Oh Snap started, what we stand for, and the people behind it. Beginners choose us for the support, guidance, and inspiration we offer whether they’re using a phone or a pro camera.

OUR VISION

To build a world where photography is not about following an endless set of rules, but about capturing true feeling. We want photography subjects seen and everyone beginner or pro to be confident and comfortable behind the lens.

OUR MISSION

To help people discover the soul of photography, not through rules but through feeling. We caption moments, not photos. We believe an excellent shot is one that speaks directly to the viewer. We’ll teach you how to set up your camera or phone to help you feel the frame not just see it.

Who Are We?

We're a team of photographers, artists, and educators who love capturing moments and helping others do the same. Our goal is to make learning photography easy and enjoyable for beginners of all ages.

Why We Created Oh Snap?

We noticed that most photography schools were too technical or hard to follow for beginners. So we created Oh Snap, a place where anyone can work with us, experience expert guidance, and get inspired every step of the way.
