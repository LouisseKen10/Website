What is OhSnap?

Oh Snap! is an engaging online photography school where beginners can learn at their own pace through expert-led sessions, helpful resources, and guided practice. Whether you use a phone or a professional camera, you’ll build the skills and confidence to take great photos from the comfort of your home.
